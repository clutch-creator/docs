Timeline



What does happen a lot in development and life? Mistakes, so we got the development part covered.

Like any other IDE, Shift offers a Undo/Redo feature, called Timeline. With navigation through the timeline of your project it is possible to rollback a previous version or even roll back to a rolled back operation, and it might get a lot more complex, but in a easy way to use.

With it's visual presentation you can:

  • Verify if what you are rolling back got synced to server
  • Check what the operation will do on rollback
  • date and author of that operation.
